Our Story

At Peliguard, our focus is protecting the American workforce. Our customers are our neighbors, and we treat every product that we produce with care knowing that it is the first line of defense. Our employees take great pains to ensure that our focus and care translates directly into the quality of our products.

Peliguard proudly assembles a full line of disposable protective equipment at our plant in Independence, Louisiana. Feel free to stop by if you are in the neighborhood.

Our Focus

Peliguard is focused on addressing the lack of transparency, reliability and accountability in the current supply chain of Personal Protective Equipment.

We accomplish this by bringing the supply of those goods back to the US. Our proprietary quality control measures and supply chain management processes allow for reliable delivery of product that can be monitored from its processing in our plant to delivery to the customer. Our processes exceed all manufacturing and quality guidelines for production. With these active measures in place, we set the benchmark for product accountability and on-time delivery.
